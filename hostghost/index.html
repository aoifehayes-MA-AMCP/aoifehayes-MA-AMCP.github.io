host/ghost

7th - 9th April 2022

St Peter’s Cork

host/ghost was a group exhibition and curatorial project by Aoife Hayes and M. Glenn Dunlea as part of the MA in Arts Management & Creative Producing programme at the School of Film, Music & Theatre, UCC.  host/ghost presents an exhibition of work by four emerging Cork-based artists. Three artists were selected via an open call process by M. Glenn Dunlea, while a fourth artist was commissioned by Aoife Hayes to produce new work in response to a brief.

host/ghost also presented two in-conversation segments with the exhibiting artists, and local activist Frank O’Connor, Co-director of the design agency anois. Recordings of these segments are available below.
