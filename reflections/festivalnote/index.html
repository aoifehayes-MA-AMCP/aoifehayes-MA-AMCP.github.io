A Note on Festival

· Aoife Hayes

I am reflecting on the concept of festival – a word that has defined the past year in many ways.

The concept of festival represents an experimental social space and undertaking. The diversity of events presented at Rebound Arts Festival demonstrates playfulness regarding perceptions of what a festival can be and what it can lead to.

Emerging from pandemic-related lockdowns, there was a sense that a celebration like Rebound Arts Festival had been delayed – which made it all the more special as it unfolded.

Something that was alluded to several times throughout the year was the feeling that a festival produces – the feeling that you are in an alternative temporal space, a space of collectivity with others. This feeling was felt strongly during Rebound Arts Festival weekend – a sense of shared responsibility, support and magic. This feeling is interdependence in action.
