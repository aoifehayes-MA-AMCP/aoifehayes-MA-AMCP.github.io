About

Aoife Hayes is a Cork-based curator from Co. Kerry. Her curatorial practice’s primary research concerns include alternative methodologies of productivity and chronopolitics.

Recent curatorial projects include; host/ghost, co-curated with Glenn Dunlea, St Peter’s Cork; CRUSH, Backwater Artists Group.

Currently completing a Masters of Arts in Arts Management & Creative Producing at University College Cork. Aoife completed her BA Joint Hons in History of Art & French from University College Cork with honours in 2017.

Her continued work in retail informs a large part of her practice.
